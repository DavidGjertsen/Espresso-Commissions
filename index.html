Timeline

Introduction

You message me and we discuss what you would like. I provide an estimate. Once we come to an agreement, I will send you an invoice via PayPal.

Sketch Phase

I work on the sketch, sending periodic updates and making changes based on your feedback. During this time you are able to cancel for a full refund.

Approval

Once the sketch is completed I will send it to you for any final changes. Once you approve the artwork is mostly locked-in, so don't waste the opportunity to make sweeping changes.

Drawing Phase

I will work towards finishing the commission, sending you periodic updates as I deem necessary or whenever you ask for them. If you cancel during this phase you will not recieve a full refund. read more.

Completion

Once your artwork is completed, I will present it for any final changes. Once I recieve payment the final high-resolution image(s) will be provided. Enjoy your beautiful new artwork.

What do you get out of it?

When all is said and done, you'll get a full-resolution JPG or GIF of your artwork. I will also include other resolutions and file types by request. If you're aiming for a specific resolution, let me know early so that I can plan ahead.

Terms and Conditions

All commissioners must be 16 or older. By commissioning me you agree to follow the following terms and conditions.

Changes

I allow large changes during the sketch phase, where I have the flexibility to make sweeping changes or start over without losing much progress. Once you approve the sketch and I move forward, making changes will be more difficult.

Small changes after the sketch is approved are fine, but larger changes may result in an additional charge depending the amount of extra work required.

Payment

An invoice will be sent via PayPal once I begin work. Full payment including any extra charges must be submitted before I will send the final high-resolution file or files.

For first-time commissioners and commissions over $50, I ask for at least 50% up front before I begin.

In the case of non-payment or chargebacks, you will be blacklisted and any artwork will be made publically available. You will still be expected to pay in full.

Cancellation and Refunds

During the sketch phase you may cancel at no cost to you, and any payment you've already sent will be refunded in full.

If you cancel after you approve the sketch, you agree to pay for whatever I finish before you cancelled. Whatever is left will be refunded, and I will clean up the unfinished artwork so you still recieve a presentable piece for your trouble.
