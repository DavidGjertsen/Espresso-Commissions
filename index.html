Heya! My name is Espresso, and I'm a digital artist and caffeine addict. My work ranges from soft boys to badass girls and everything in between.

My go-to aesthetic is cute boys in hoodies, but I draw all kinds of things! I love having the chance to try something new, so I'll take on (most) anything thrown my way.

Additional Information

I will draw:

  • All species, including furries, humans, fantasy races, aliens, etc
  • OCs and fan characters, whether yours or mine
  • Fanart of certain characters (Ask me)
  • Suggestive poses and outfits or implied / artistic nudity
  • Complex backgrounds and scenes
  • Light violence or gore
  • Some heavy topics and themes (Ask me)

I will not draw:

  • NFT-generated characters
  • Outright NSFW content
  • "SFW" fetish content
  • Depictions of extreme violence or gore
  • Artwork promoting hate or discrimination

Timeline

Completion

What you get

You will recieve a full-resolution file in JPG or GIF format. You are also able to request:

  • Alternate images, resolutions, and file types.
  • A timelapse video of the drawing process.

Inquiries

If you'd like to inquire about a commission, or have any other questions about me and my work, please to get in touch! I can be reached via email, espressothewolf@gmail.com or on Twitter, @EspressoTheWolf.

Please include:

  • A detailed description of the artwork you would like to commission.
  • Any visual references you wish to provide.
  • An email to which I can send payment information.

Please ensure you have read and agree to my terms of service before submitting an inquiry.

Terms of Service

General Terms

  • All commissioners must be 18 or older.
  • Visual references are to be provided at the time of inquiry. An inconvenience fee may be added if references are not provided.
  • Deadlines, if any, must be made clear in the inquiry. An additional cost may be added for rush work.
  • The commissioner must have permission to use all characters included in the inquiry.
  • Inquiries containing anything in my "Will not draw" list will be ignored, and the commissioner may be blacklisted.

Payment

  • Payment will be through PayPal invoice.
  • First-time commissioners and commissions priced at $60 or more will be required to submit at least 50% payment up-front.
  • Additional charges may be added for complexity, extra characters, etc.
  • Full payment, including any additional charges, must be recieved before the commissioner recieves their final file(s).
  • Failure to pay, charging back, or attempting to dispute the payment will result in the commissioner being blacklisted.
  • Payment using cryptocurrencies will not be accepted, and the commissioner will be blacklisted.

Changes

  • The commissioner will be sent a sketch for changes and approval before moving on to finishing stages.
  • Major changes may be made prior to approval. Major changes after approval may incur an additional cost depending on the amount of work involved.
  • Minor changes may be made at any stage in the process.

Refunds and Cancellation

  • I may refuse or cancel any commission at my discretion. If I cancel for any reason, the commissioner will recieve a full refund.
  • The commissioner may cancel prior to approval of the sketch for a full refund.
  • The commissioner will recieve a partial refund for cancellation after approval of the sketch, based on the amount of work completed at the time of cancellation.

Rights and Agreements

  • I reserve all rights to the commissioned artwork, including use for personal promotion, advertising, portfolio work, etc.
  • The commissioner may use commissioned artwork for personal use, including icons, banners, public postings, personal branding, etc.
  • The commissioner may NOT use commissioned artwork for commercial use, such as merchandise.
  • The commissioner may NOT tokenize the artwork.
  • The commissioner may NOT edit the artwork, nor remove or obscure my watermark, nor claim the commissioned work as their own artwork.
  • The commissioner is expected to provide credit where reasonable. Please credit the artist as EspressoTheWolf or @EspressoTheWolf.
  • If commissioned artwork is traded, resold, or otherwise changes hands, the new owner(s) must agree to these terms.

Thank you!
